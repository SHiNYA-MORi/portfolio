About

He is Shinya Mori. He seeks pluriverse through technology and art. He explore plural futures where people with diverse values respect each other and embrace non-anthropocentric perspectives. He is based on open / free culture, and eastern philosophy like animism, taoism, and buddhism. He basically works in public goods space and commons. He focuses on mostly coordination mechanisms; public goods funding, impact evaluation, collective decision making, governance of commons, credible nutrality and decentralized democratic governance. And then, he lives in the positive sum game and the infinite game.
