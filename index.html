Co-founder of imi and Beacon Labs. He explores plural futures where people with diverse values respect each other and embrace non-anthropocentric perspectives. His work is grounded in open/free culture and eastern philosophy—animism, taoism, and buddhism—that honors interconnectedness beyond human-centric worldviews. He works in public goods and commons, focusing on coordination mechanisms that enable collective action, public goods funding mechanisms, and impact evaluation frameworks. He expresses his thought as artworks, research, and other activities.
